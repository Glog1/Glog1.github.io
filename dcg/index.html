SMO - Social Media
Optimization

SMO tool allows you to generate a high conversion of attendance of the official site of the company through promotion in social networks.

SMO optimizes the site for promotion tasks in SOCIAL MEDIA, corrects the content of the brand group at all venues (VK, Facebook, Instagram, YOUTUBE, OK) and creates a strong "link link" between the social network and the site.

How we work with SMO:

1 Stage - Analysis

  • We estimate the quality of social networks management (content, relevance of CA, number of publications, KPI indicators, etc.)
  • We compose a portrait of targeted subscribers
  • We analyze communities of competitive brands
  • We form an analytical report in the form of a presentation

2 Stage - STRATEGY

  • We select social networks for SMO-promotion
  • Define the geolocation of promotion
  • We formulate the goals of promotion (promotion to the top of the Google / Yandex search engine for key queries, promotion to the top search engine of the social network for key queries, traffic to the site, leads, subscribers, coverage, involvement)
  • We select algorithms for the implementation of SMO-strategy: targeting, inviting, interactive mechanics, "massfollowing", etc.
  • Define KPI and budget

Stage 3 - STARTING AND CONDUCTING

  • Optimize the promoted community for SMO tasks
  • Optimize the site (add launchers, simulate the code)
  • We start the work on the promotion of communities

How we work with SMM:

1 Stage - Analytics

  • We analyze the current SMM brand activity (existing groups, official pages, content calculations)
  • Looking at the user activity in the SMM channels of the brand
  • We select the most effective social networks for conducting
  • We formulate the goals and objectives of SMM-management, collecting them in a brief analytical report (leads, subscribers, coverage, involvement, image, etc.).

2 Stage - Concept

  • We create and describe in detail the concept of the community with the broadcast on all selected social networks (the idea, the portrait of the future subscriber, the basic values, the rhetoric of addressing the subscriber)
  • Create rubrics and a logical rubricator
  • We show approximate examples of posts and registration of the community (graphic style and text)

3 Step - Start

  • Create a content plan (weekly publication plan)
  • We create the first 10 publications according to the approved rubricator (for approval)
  • Create a corporate identity and description
  • Start the maintenance according to the content plan

4 Stage - Maintaining

  • Implementation of the content strategy
  • Implementation of the promotion strategy
  • Monthly report on KPI
